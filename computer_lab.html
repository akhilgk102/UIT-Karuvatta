Computer Lab

Our college computer lab is a well-equipped and modern facility designed to support the academic and practical needs of students. It houses 25 high-performance computers, each installed with the latest software and tools required for programming, web development, and other computer science-related tasks. The lab provides a conducive environment for learning, with high-speed internet access and adequate seating arrangements. It serves as a hub for practical sessions, project development, and research, ensuring students gain hands-on experience to complement their theoretical knowledge. The lab is maintained with utmost care to ensure all systems function seamlessly, fostering an ideal space for innovation and skill enhancement.
