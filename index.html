About Us

Innovative Way To Learn

University Institute Of Technology, Karuvatta, located in Kerala was established in 2012. The institute offers various UG and PG programs, offered in Full Time mode. The faculty at the University Institute Of Technology, Karuvatta is well-experienced with years of experience. The institute provides courses in various streams including Accounting & Commerce, Science and IT & Software. University Institute Of Technology, Karuvatta provides students with an opportunity to learn from skilled and experienced faculty members. In total, the institute provides 225 seats for the students. The infrastructure at the institute is well-built with modern facilities.
