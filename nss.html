National Service Scheme (NSS)

About NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at UIT College is a vibrant platform for students to contribute towards societal welfare. NSS aims at developing students' personalities through community service, fostering a sense of social responsibility, and instilling leadership qualities.

NSS volunteers actively participate in activities such as community clean-ups, awareness campaigns, and blood donation drives, making a positive impact on society.

  • Focus on social service and personality development
  • Active involvement in community welfare programs
  • Opportunities to lead and collaborate with peers

NSS Activities

Cleanliness Drive

NSS volunteers actively organize campus and community clean-up campaigns to promote hygiene and cleanliness.

Enviromental Day

"On Environmental Day, our college hosts a tree-planting drive to foster sustainability and green initiatives."

Awareness Campaigns

Campaigns on health, education, and environment are organized to educate and empower the community.
